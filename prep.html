ABOUT


Tom Havelock

[Vocalist, Lyricist]

 Tom Havelock, also known by his stage name Tom Cane, is the lead vocalist of the PREP band.
Havelock has a diverse musical discography, including collaborations with various artists in writing and producing.





Llywelyn Ap Myrddin

[Keyboardist]

Llywelyn ap Myrddin is a pianist, composer and producer. He has written works ranging from opera
“An original mind at work” to solo piano and is currently part of modern city pop group PREP





Guillaume Jambel

[Drummer]

Guillaume Jambel, also known by Giom, is no stranger in the music field.
He's made his mark as a member of musical groups like PREP, Anonimaly, and Paperlung, bringing his unique flair to the industry.
He has also headlined and played at some of the world’s best clubs such as Fabric (London), Privilege (Ibiza), LIV (Buenos Aires), 360 (Dubai), performing in over 30 countries.





Dan Radclyffe

[Producer, Guitarist]

Dan Radclyffe has collaborated with Tom Havelock on previous writing projects, working with artists such as Sinead Hartnett, Riton, and Ray BLK.
With their collective passion, the four members of PREP continue to cultivate their unique sound.
